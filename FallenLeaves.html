FALLEN LEAVES ON PATHS TRAVERSED
×

Watercolour / year 2015 11 inches X 16 inches

Oftentimes I look back,
at the years gone by, and ponder.
There were roads I took, which I should not have taken.
And roads I ignored, which I should have taken.
There were paths I took in haste, without thinking.
And there were paths, which I never valued.
There were lanes I missed.
And there were turns I took, with my eyes closed.
There were doors I knocked on, which never opened.
And, there were open doors, which I did not see.
Some paths led me to dead ends.
Some paths led me to desolate gardens.
And yet, some paths led me to beautiful horizons.
I ponder oftentimes....
Should I have taken this road or knocked on that door?
Should I have waited, and rationalised calmly?
And then, slicing through the wet fog,
Like a lostvagabond leaf, in the winter breeze.
A voice comes haunting and I realise...
Every turn I took, every turn I missed.
Every road I walked on & every path I ignored.
Every lane I rushed into, every dead end.
Every desolate garden & every locked door.
It brought me here...
Destiny has its own designs.
Decisions were mine.
This must be the greatest illusion of mine!
