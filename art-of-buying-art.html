Art Of Buying Art

Buying art is a very personal, a very thoughtful and mindful action. When you buy art and bring it home, what you are effectively doing is bringing an energy home. That energy will occupy a space in your house. Hence the art must speak to you. The energy embodied in the art must interact with your thoughts. And should bring healing and good “vastu” (aura) of the elements of Universe into your room. The painting on your wall is a doorway that connects you to your experiences. It is a window that brings in the winds of healing and hope and joy. It is a dream where your soul finds refuge. Art on your wall is not just a lifeless picture. It is alive and throbbing part of your home energy. In my art catalogue (Timeline 2013 to 2019), I bring to you the healing energy of seasons, colours, forests and nature and the strength of the soul. I believe that God, Angels, Saints, Divinity, all reside in nature. I bring to you the beauty of the Divine Symphony of the Universe.
