A Connection
×

Watercolor/ year 2019
( Watercolour vignettes are interesting because for me they develop like stories from my novel.
Birds embody souls and ancestors and soul journeys. They are carriers of messages from celestial realms.
Crows are soul birds from ancestors.)

He saw her perched on a dead branch.
She seemed sweet, like a doll.
He didn't see the nest that lay hidden, Under that dead branch.
His flamboyance made her smile.
She peered at him, fascinated.
Wanting to know a little about his thoughts.
The fire in him was enticing.
And his innocence was sweet.
He looked keenly.
Not a fool she was, he knew.
Yet her naivete was alluring.
Calm yet restless she seemed.
The way she gazed at him, caught his heart.
The story had potential.
The skies were romantic.
Colours of autumn, cruelly passionate.
Who would express the intention.
The intent that the eyes spoke...
The unspoken floats like letters on clouds and leaves.
Oh well, it is just a landscape in my heart,
That found its way into the brush.
But , were I to remove a bird from the picture,
It sure gets lonely there, despite the beauty & structure.
Soul ties they say, are not an easy juncture.
He is there and she looks,
It makes the picture beautiful....
