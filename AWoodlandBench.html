A Woodland Bench
×

Watercolour/ year 2010
(Style - Early Impressionism)

How oft have I passed these silent woodland benches.
Saw tiny birds fly and chirp,
And sunlight peep through tree windows.
Nothing changes yet everything changed...
This is my home, the Himalayan Foothills. My Bungalow is amidst an oak forest. This is a tiny Oak Grove behind an old Church built by the Britishers.
Hardly anyone has the time to come and sit on these quaint old benches nowadays. World has moved into rooms and into television sets.
But yes, exotic birds from distant lands do come to see the spring flowers even today. :) And my canine babies love to roll and run around the trees and play.
