PAINTING THE SOUL

I am an artist and a published author. My art book "Meandering Brush" on my paintings of soul landscapes and poetry & thought has been published worldwide. My primary medium is oil and water colours. I am a self taught artist. I learned art in the school of life. I learned art by studying the Masters. A few contemporary artists from Europe have guided and mentored me in my art journey. I do soul landscapes. My paintings of nature and forests embody a colour aura and emotional atmosphere. I work with the energy of colours, forests, light and shadow and wind. My style is impressionism with a surrealist aura aligned to European & Russian art styles. My art brings in the healing of forests and is meditative. My published art book Meandering Brush, art book on my catalogue of paintings and poems is available with Amazon, Kindle, Notion Press. My art is aligned to Forest healing. My soul landscapes express the deep connect between nature and art. When Art embodies the energy of nature, art becomes meditative and healing.

My Art Catalogue

Elemental Soulscapes

A SoulScape is both an extraordinary work of art and a process of self-discovery. It is an oil-on-canvas painting that captures the ‘inspired you’ – the inner qualities that make you truly unique.

Concept Portraits

Portrait is a genre in painting, where the intent is to represent a specific human subject. The purpose of a concept portrait is to create art for which the idea behind the portrait is more important than the finished art portrait.

Landscapes

Landscapes are works of art that feature scenes of nature. This includes mountains, lakes, gardens, rivers, and any scenic view. Landscapes can be oil paintings, watercolors, acrylic or prints of any kind.

Vingettes

A vignette is a short piece of art which expresses very clearly and neatly the typical characteristics of the thing that it represents. It has no definite border, and shades off gradually at the edges into the background.
MEANDERING BRUSH

Meandering Brush is a journey of the soul. It is a marriage of beautiful landscapes and verses that express emotions in all their myriad hues. The soulful paintings and poignant verses tell the story of many hearts. A story of love in all its hues. Soul heals with the stimulus it receives from the 5 senses. Sight is one of them. Aligned with the concept of “healing the soul”, these soul landscapes and verses spread the powerful and healing energy of colours and elements of nature through art. This is a one of its kind coffee table book exhibit of artwork and poetry. The book offers a collection of beautiful paintings under the theme of ‘Soul Landscapes” that bring the healing energy of nature and colours into your environment. And the soulful poems give wings to the heart. Art mirrors life. And life is expressed through art. Meandering Brush, through its winding forests and colours, woven by the artist’s brush, make so many feelings come alive. As the writer-artist Nehab says, “Regrets there are none. It is just a winter mood. Wine is passion red. Sky is stormy blue.” Absorb the power of emotions.
