PAINTING THE SOUL

I am an artist and a published author. My art book "Meandering Brush" on my paintings of soul landscapes and poetry & thought has been published worldwide. My primary medium is oil and water colours. I am a self taught artist. I learned art in the school of life. I learned art by studying the Masters. I do soul landscapes. My paintings of nature and forests embody a colour aura and emotional atmosphere. I work with the energy of colours, forests, light and shadow and wind. My style is impressionism with a surrealist aura aligned to European & Russian art styles. My art brings in the healing of forests and is meditative. My published art book Meandering Brush, an art catalogue of paintings and poems is available with Amazon, Kindle, Notion Press. My art is aligned to Forest healing. My soul landscapes express the deep connect between nature and art. When Art embodies the energy of nature, art becomes meditative and healing. My art is inspired by the journey of my life. Each painting that I have created is a story I have lived. I feel the resonance of my feelings in the elements of nature and Universe. Hence, I tell my story and express my feelings in the colours of nature through the meanderings of my brush and my pen.

My Art Catalogue

Soulscape of Elements

Fire, water, earth, aether, air, wind, clouds, sunshine, moonlight, all elements have a soul energy. Surrealist in genre, Soulscapes of Elements are my paintings that capture the soul of the forces of the Universe and weave them into stories of my heart.

Vignettes

Vignettes are small paintings, that are like little stories on the wall. They are illustrative in genre and are like pages of a novel.

Mood Landscapes

Mood Landscapes capture the aura of seasons and embody the healing vibe of nature in all its hues and moods. Impressionist in genre, my Mood Landscapes carry a distinct colour and kinetic energy. They are moments frozen in time, turned into portals of soul travel on the wall, like eternal windows.

Concept Portraits

Concept portraits are emotive portraiture in fauvist and expressionist genre. These are portraits of emotions. The muse in concept portraits is the Alluring Feminine.

Commissioned Projects

Photos when turned into paintings,become alive with a soul energy. A few paintings created as commissioned projects.

Commissioned painting pointers:

  1. The picture should speak to me and my style should speak to you.
  2. The fixed and the changable aspects of the picture to be prestated.(changes can be worked in even after the painting is complete, in case of oil medium).
  3. Time period as per the dynamics of oil, water or acrylic.
  4. Monetary details - negotiable.
  5. My style is impressionism, Peredvizniki Realism, tonalist style, colour surrealism.
  6. I am a landscape artist. I do nature and architechtural landscapes and still life
  7. I do not do portraits or figurative.
MEANDERING BRUSH

Meandering Brush is a journey of the soul. It is a marriage of beautiful landscapes and verses that express emotions in all their myriad hues. The soulful paintings and poignant verses tell the story of many hearts. A story of love in all its hues. Soul heals with the stimulus it receives from the 5 senses. Sight is one of them. Aligned with the concept of “healing the soul”, these soul landscapes and verses spread the powerful and healing energy of colours and elements of nature through art. This is a one of its kind coffee table book exhibit of artwork and poetry. The book offers a collection of beautiful paintings under the theme of ‘Soul Landscapes” that bring the healing energy of nature and colours into your environment. And the soulful poems give wings to the heart. Art mirrors life. And life is expressed through art. Meandering Brush, through its winding forests and colours, woven by the artist’s brush, make so many feelings come alive. As the writer-artist Nehab says, “Regrets there are none. It is just a winter mood. Wine is passion red. Sky is stormy blue.” Absorb the power of emotions.
