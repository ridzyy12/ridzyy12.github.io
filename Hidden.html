HIDDEN
×

Oil on Canvas / year 2015
Medium/ 18 inches x 20 inches

Hidden in the womb of a forest,
Lies a beautiful sanctuary.
Where hues of love colour the landscape.
And memories float like leaves on water.
Where peace and harmony resides like a symphony.
Where questions cease and answers unfold.
Where silence speaks and sound is silent.
To find that sanctuary is the journey.
A journey of courage through dark forests.
It is hidden deep in your soul.
Behind veils and dense fogs.
Behind darkness and lighted illusions.
To reach it, is a journey of many lifetimes...
It lies hidden
Deep within.
