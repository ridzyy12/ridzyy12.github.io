BLUE MOON

(The Illusion she loved..)

×

Watercolour/ year 2015

He came from distant lands.
Shores she had never seen
He came like a dream...
A dream she had always seen.
He crossed her path,
Like a twist in the tale.
Caught her off guard.
Ruthlessly ensnared.
A quirk of destiny,
She couldn’t foretell.
She couldn’t see it coming.
The potent love spell.
She saw her Prince, the light was behind.
She couldn’t see, brightness blinded her eyes.
She wrapped him in the silvery cloak of her dream.
The cloak was not his at all,this she couldn’t see.
She couldn’t see his cloak was dark,
Hiding secrets within.
She created an illusion and loved her dream.
She covered him in the silver dust of her desires.
The moon cast a magical light.
The stars shone so bright.
She loved the illusion she had created.
She loved her blue moon dream.
