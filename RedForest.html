RED FOREST
×

Watercolour on Paper/ year 2013
Medium / 20 inches x 16 inches

As the red Maple sheds its leaves.
The blood of autumn falls like drops.
In the Red Forest,
Etched in blood,
Hang from trees,
Float in the breeze,
A thousand stories of loves lived and lost.
The little tree window in a red forest,
Looks heavenwards,
Filled with love,
Lost or sought...
