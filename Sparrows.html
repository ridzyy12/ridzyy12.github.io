Sparrows
×

Oil painting / year 2014

This is my copy of Micheal Dumas's "The Immigrants" (2003)... It is one of my personal study artwork I did. I was picking up the brush after a long hiatus. I just loved the love in this. And so wanted to paint it for myself. Of course I did it "my way".
These "immigrants" sparrows have an interesting story attached to them too...
The green-shuttered window belongs to the old Keene Hotel in the historic village at Lang, Ontario. The two house sparrows seen here, perched on the window-sill have a nest in the summer shed of the Fitzpatrick house next door. The ancestors of both the birds and the human builders of the village came to the 'new world', where through their toughness and resourcefulness they thrived and grew. This was all in 2003. Wonder if their children are still there...
Sparrows embody soul & passion... Sparrow is a sacred bird to Aphrodite, the goddess of love, symbolising true love, passion and spiritual connection.
