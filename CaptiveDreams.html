AWARENESS OF CAPTIVE DREAMS
×

(year 2018)

You don’t see me, you don’t hear me.
I am Just a number in some census.
My songs echo in dark rooms.
Where no light ushers in, where no breeze blows.
The key lies lost, somewhere in the blackness.
When I close my eyes, I see a beautiful world.
Where sunlight shines like diamond.
I see golden fields swaying beneath azure skies.
I see frost paint patterns in white.
I hear the waves crashing against cliffs and shores.
But you don’t see me, you don’t hear me.
The key lies lost, somewhere in the blackness.
To find the key, is the journey.
A day will come when my songs will find wings.
And you will see the soul you never valued.
Darkness is not forever.
Light always breaks through...
