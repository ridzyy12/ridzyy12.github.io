RAIN ON THE HIGHWAY
×

Watercolour / year 2018 16.5 inches x 23.5 inches

Rain & wind, lashed hard.
Raged like fire.
Surreal was the light.
Unlike day, unlike night.
Music in the car echoed smooth.
It was the red spot in front that caught my eye.
It made me think.
It made my mind wander -
To lanes and alleys long forgotten.
To all that had gone by.
Raindrops ran down, mixing with tears.
I was looking in front and seeing within.
Hypnotised by the red spot in front.
It refracted & reflected.
Shone like a ruby.
Blood red.
Just like the red forest I had left behind.
Yonder, in the horizon, twinkled a grey labyrinth of wilderness.
Juxtaposed I was between two jungles.
The weather invaded my eyes.
It was the red spot on the road.
That caught my eye.
Journey was not over yet.
Twist in the tale was just round the corner.
